Our Story

From a single garage to island-wide.

Car Rental started in 2012 when our founder Kamal Perera noticed that tourists and locals alike were stuck with overpriced, unreliable vehicle options. He leased a small garage in Colombo 3 and put 12 cars on the road. Word spread fast.

Today we operate 8 pickup hubs across Sri Lanka — Colombo, Kandy, Galle, Negombo, Jaffna, Trincomalee, Anuradhapura, and Matara — with door-to-door delivery available islandwide.
